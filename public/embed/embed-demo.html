This page demonstrates how to embed the ThreeDotts AI conversational widget into any website. The widget appears in the bottom-right corner and allows visitors to start voice conversations with your AI agent.

Copy and paste this code into your website's HTML to add the widget:

<!-- ThreeDotts AI Widget --> <script src="https://yourdomain.com/embed/threedotts-widget.js" data-agent-id="your-agent-id-here"></script>

Note: Replace your-agent-id-here with your actual ElevenLabs agent ID, and update the script src URL to point to your hosted widget file.
